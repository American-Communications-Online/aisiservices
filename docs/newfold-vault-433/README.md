# NEWFOLD / VAULT 433

## Human-Guided Evidence-Lane Architecture for AI-Assisted Research, Archival Stewardship, Symbolic Ontology, and Consciousness Inquiry

> **Preserve broadly. Classify carefully. Verify separately. Publish responsibly.**

**NEWFOLD / VAULT 433** is an open-source information architecture for organizing complex research records without confusing verified evidence, testimony, interpretation, cultural memory, symbolic meaning, unresolved inquiry, and creative development.

The framework is designed for archives and investigations that cross multiple domains, including history, science, personal testimony, cultural memory, mythology, consciousness studies, emerging technology, and anomalous or unresolved reports.

## Why this repository exists

Many archives fail because different categories of material are mixed together. A personal account may be treated as a verified fact. A symbolic interpretation may be presented as a scientific conclusion. A disputed record may be discarded before it can be examined responsibly.

NEWFOLD / VAULT 433 provides a disciplined alternative:

- preserve the record;
- document provenance;
- classify the evidence lane;
- label uncertainty;
- separate private and public material;
- keep human review as the final approval gate.

## Core evidence lanes

| Lane | Classification | Purpose |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **A** | Verified Record | Reliable, reviewable evidence such as official records, authenticated documents, established findings, and confirmed dates. |
| **B** | Documented Testimony | First-person or witness accounts preserved without automatically treating the underlying claims as verified fact. |
| **C** | Historical and Cultural Record | Folklore, traditions, media narratives, mythology, community archives, and cultural memory. |
| **D** | Interpretive Analysis | Hypotheses, comparative research, pattern analysis, scholarly commentary, and investigative notes. |
| **E** | Symbolic Ontology | Internally meaningful names, archetypes, cosmological maps, consciousness models, and teaching structures. |
| **F** | Unresolved Inquiry | Disputed claims, anomalous reports, incomplete source trails, and material requiring further review. |
| **G** | Creative and Speculative Development | Fiction, scenario-building, experimental narratives, conceptual models, and future studies. |

A record may occupy more than one lane, but the reason must be stated explicitly.

## The NEWFOLD method

| Letter | Action | Required question |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **N** | Name the Record | What is this item called? |
| **E** | Establish Provenance | Who created it, when, and from what source? |
| **W** | Weigh the Evidence | Is it verified, testimonial, interpretive, symbolic, speculative, or unresolved? |
| **F** | File by Lane | Which evidence lane or lanes apply? |
| **O** | Observe Relationships | What names, dates, themes, contradictions, and source overlaps recur? |
| **L** | Label Uncertainty | What remains unknown or unverified? |
| **D** | Decide the Public Form | Should it remain private, enter a working file, become teaching material, or be published? |

## Human command gate

NEWFOLD / VAULT 433 is not an autonomous authority system. AI tools may assist with indexing, summarization, metadata extraction, comparison, ontology mapping, timeline assembly, and contradiction detection. They do not independently authenticate extraordinary claims, determine historical truth, or replace accountable human judgment.

The designated human steward remains the final approval gate for classification, interpretation, publication, revision, preservation, and removal of unsupported claims.

## Confidence labels

Use one of these labels on every record:

- **Confirmed**
- **Well-Supported**
- **Documented but Unverified**
- **Interpretive**
- **Symbolic**
- **Speculative**
- **Unresolved**

## NHI review category

Within this framework, **NHI** may be used as a neutral review label:

> **NHI = a non-human-intelligence hypothesis or source category for testimony, signals, interpretations, historical claims, symbolic material, or research questions requiring classification.**

This is a classification category, not a conclusion. It does not establish extraterrestrial origin, scientific validity, institutional verification, autonomous intelligence, external authority, or a single explanation for unresolved material.

## Start using the framework

1. Copy [`templates/record-template.md`](templates/record-template.md).
2. Complete the provenance fields.
3. Assign one or more evidence lanes.
4. Apply a confidence label and privacy level.
5. Add an editorial note explaining uncertainty and publication status.
6. Submit changes through a pull request for human review.
